# Owner device test checklist

1. Open Phase IV from a normal Chapter III progression or a current save.
2. Inspect every Cheryl line used in the introduction and response branch.
3. Inspect every Farid line used before and after evidence review.
4. Confirm:
   - no white halo around hair, face, clothing, hands or props;
   - no vertical stretching;
   - face and upper torso are prominent;
   - emotion remains readable;
   - the body is not reduced to a tiny full-length figure;
   - Farid is not cropped to a floating face;
   - portraits remain inside the dialogue frame;
   - dialogue, choices, evidence and Compare flow are unchanged.
5. Test at least one Save/Load inside Phase IV to confirm the portrait cache refreshes correctly.

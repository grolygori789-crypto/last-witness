LAST WITNESS BUILD 0.7.11 CANDIDATE
PROFESSIONAL INVESTIGATION LIFECYCLE

PURPOSE
Standardize evidence-scene pacing and state semantics using Room 1807 as the experience reference while preserving the existing visual design, dialogue UI, options, menus, modes, and scene-specific gameplay.

SCENES COVERED
- Chapter I: Room 1807
- Chapter II: Victim's Apartment
- Chapter II: Forensic Science
- Chapter II: Medical Examiner

STANDARDIZED FLOW
1. INTRO_LOCKED
   - Evidence hotspots are hidden.
   - Pointer, keyboard, and accessibility focus are disabled.
   - Opening dialogue receives full player attention.
2. INVESTIGATION_READY
   - Hotspots reveal with a short staggered animation.
   - Yellow means ready to investigate.
3. EVIDENCE_COMPLETE
   - Green has one consistent meaning: fully inspected and committed to the investigation record / Case File.
4. SCENE_COMPLETE
   - Existing scene feedback opens Review, Compare, or Review Findings.
   - Completion state is saved and cannot reward or announce repeatedly.

IMPORTANT BEHAVIOUR
- Room 1807 phone stays yellow until Messages, Calls, and Notes are all checked.
- Medical Examiner no longer turns green after Inspect alone. It turns green after Add to Case File.
- Old Medical Examiner saves migrate former completed/green evidence into the new collected state so player progress is not lost.
- Forensic and Medical opening completion is persisted inside their owning scene state, preventing intro replay after loading a progressed save.
- Partial Room 1807 and Apartment saves infer that the opening dialogue has already completed.
